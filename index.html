How It Works

Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation (DCKV) systems transform your traditional kitchen hood into an intelligent, automated ventilation system that responds to actual cooking activity rather than running at full speed constantly.

Smart Sensors

Our sensors placed in your kitchen hood monitor temperature changes and smoke generated by your cooking equipment in real-time.

Automatic Operation

Based on temperature sensor readings, the system automatically gauges the level of smoke produced by cooking equipment.

Fan Speed Control

When cooking activity is low, the our exhaust speed controller automatically reduces fan speed.

Energy Savings in Action

Field studies suggest that energy savings range from $2K-$10K per year by running fans only as fast as needed.

The result? Your kitchen ventilation works smarter, not harder – automatically adjusting to save energy while keeping your kitchen properly ventilated and safe.

Where the Savings Come From

Space Heating

By exhausting less air, you can keep more warm air in the building.

Electrical

By running your exhaust fan slower, you will pay less on hydro bills.

Rebates

Green-energy rebates save you money on the capital cost of installation.

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for our Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation system, your restaurant must meet the following criteria:

Independent Ownership

The restaurant owner operates less than 10 restaurant locations in Ontario.

Ventilation System

Your restaurant must have a natural gas fueled Make-Up Air (MUA) system.

Consumption

Annual natural gas consumption must be less than 100,000 cubic meters based on a three year average.
