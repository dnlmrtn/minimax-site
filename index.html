How It Works

Demand Control Kitchen Ventilation (DCKV) systems transform your traditional kitchen hood into an intelligent, automated ventilation system that responds to actual cooking activity rather than running at full speed constantly.

Smart Sensors

Our system uses advanced sensors strategically placed in your kitchen hood to continuously monitor cooking conditions. These sensors detect temperature changes, humidity levels, and smoke generated by your cooking equipment in real-time.

Variable Speed Fan Control

Based the temperature sensor readings, motor controllers automatically make adjustments to the speed of your exhaust fan motors. When your exhaust motors previously could only turn on and off, they now work like a dimmer switch on a light, changing anywhere from 0-100%.

Automatic Operation

When cooking activity is light or absent, the system automatically reduces fan speed to the minimum required level. When sensors detect smoke or other airborne contaminants, they trigger the variable frequency drives to adjust the exhaust fan speed to ensure adequate evacuation.

Energy Savings in Action

Field studies suggest that energy savings could be 60% or more depending on the facility and type of operation. By running fans only as fast as needed, the system dramatically reduces energy consumption while maintaining proper ventilation and safety standards.

The result? Your kitchen ventilation works smarter, not harder – automatically adjusting to save energy while keeping your kitchen properly ventilated and safe.
